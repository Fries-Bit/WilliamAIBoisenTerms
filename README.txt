By using William Ai Boisen (referred to as “WAIB”), you agree that your interactions may be logged and used to improve the system.

You can choose whether to participate in this:

If you’re okay with helping improve WAIB, your messages may be stored and reviewed to help the system learn especially when new or unfamiliar words or phrasing are used.
If you prefer not to have your messages logged, you can opt out at any time.

How to opt in or out:

Type /optout to stop your messages from being logged and or inspected by WAIB.
Type /optin if you’d like to contribute again and help improve WAIB.

Please note: if you opt back in, your messages will once again be stored and used for improvement.

Thank you.
